Lambda calculus have 3 basic components / terms:
    - Expressions
    - Variables
    - Abstractions

Combinators - A lambda term with no free Variables

Divergence - when the reduction process never ends (loop)

Reducible Expressions - Redexes. Also called Evaluation/reduction.
    - also called Normalizing or executing an Expressions

Parameter / argument - the values that are passed into a function

Lists are composed of "Elements"
